About

Why for?

Chordly was created to support musicians – begginers, advanced and those in the midway 😁 BUT especially for my wife, the originator and supporter 😍

Chordly is an app that was created to hone your skills. Chordly will force you to expect the unexpected and help you react spontaneously to every chord changes. You will be able to play smoother and more confident.

I think it's a great excercise and hope you will like it too. You can use Chordly on any device that has internet connection. You can simply put your phone on your piano or on your music stand.

What's inside?

Chordly was made with lots of 💖 and: HTML, CSS, pure JavaScript, Git and GitHub. You can see yourself how it's made – a repo, it's public.
