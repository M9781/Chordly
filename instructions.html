Instructions

How does it work?

Chordly is made for practicing chords. Are you getting bored with the same, well known patterns? I think you should give it a try. Accept challenge to open up for the unexpected stream of random chords!

Personalize

You can start practicing chords right away with default settings, but wouldn't it be nicer to play with your own rules? Choose visibility of features, set a perfect tempo and repeat chords as much as you like. Chordly will remember your settings on the same device – if you come back – it's still here.

Choose chords

Wanna try something harder or perfect your skills? Just check chords you like.

Get it started

Are you ready? No worries, you can adjust your settings on the fly. Just click start button, prepare instrument, watch the countdown end and play! Have enough? Just click the pause button.

More details?

Chordly allows you to choose the time signature for metronome. You can turn it off if you'd like to practice all alone. You can use Chordly on any device that has internet connection. You can simply put your phone on your piano or on your music stand. Too small to focus? A fullscreen mode is just for you.
